# Chapter 1: Introduction to Physical AI

**Part of**: Weeks 1-2 - Foundation (Introduction to Physical AI)  
**Course Structure**: This chapter covers the foundational weeks before Module 1 begins.

Welcome to the first chapter of the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics textbook! This chapter establishes the foundational concepts you'll need for all subsequent chapters.

## üéØ Chapter Overview

Physical AI represents the convergence of artificial intelligence with the physical world through robotics. Unlike software-only AI that operates in digital environments, Physical AI systems must perceive, reason about, and interact with the real world in real-time.

## üìö What You'll Learn

In this chapter, you will:

1. **Understand Physical AI** - Learn what makes embodied AI different from traditional software AI
2. **Recognize Key Challenges** - Understand real-time constraints, sensor noise, and safety requirements
3. **Master Sensor & Actuator Basics** - Learn how robots perceive and act in the world
4. **Start Python Robotics Programming** - Build your first robot simulations with NumPy and Matplotlib
